Membership

Whether it's keeping up with the kids at the park, getting out and meeting new people, or even landing the next promotion at work, your workout can give you the confidence to get more out of your life.

That is why Big Gym has changed. We want gym to be more accessible for everyone, so we are now offering flexible membership terms and even more options suit the way you train. With our world-class facilities, extended working hours, group training options and expert team - we will show you how you can get ahead and stay there.


Standard membership pay your membership dues monthly (initiation fee may apply)
Loyalty Rewards pay an initiation fee and receive discounted monthly dues when compared to the standard membership. Receive a reduction in ongoing dues after year 1.
Commitment Membership Get a lower rate when you commit to 12 months of membership compared to the standard monthly payment membership. After the 12 month commitment, your membership continues on a monthly payment basis.
Loyalty Prepay prepay discounted dues for a set period of time and then convert to convenient monthly payments. Monthly dues paid via electronic fund transfer (EFT) begins after your prepaid period of time
Limited Term Prepay Pre-pay for your membership. We offer a variety of pre-paid memberships from as little as 30 days up to 3 years.
